What is E-cell?

“Entrepreneurship-cell” it is cell just like the cells of life which deals with minute aspects leading bright minds to create and build new ideas inculcating into startups or securing good jobs and thus to reach the pinnacle of a success. Thus providing an overall learning, gathering, and execution based system to get a higher than ever student success ratio of our future graduates.

About

When you cease to dream you cease to live” - Malcolm Forbes, chairman and editor in chief of Forbes Magazine.

NSEC Entrepreneurship-Cell

We a group of visionaries had set forth on the venture of creating innovating and harnessing the true potential of the students ion as any singularity but as a self-sustainable community so as to not only enhance but creating a better future by developing eminent leadership and high work attributes, thus being prepared to combat the waves of opportunity led by life.

Vision and Mission of E-Cell

To enhance the startup culture as well as job profile evaluation on basis of learning gathering and execution based system that we at the NSEC e-cell hope to deploy to achieve our set goals in a definite period of time working in a healthy and efficient manner.

INDUCTION SEMINAR

INDUCTION SEMINAR

21st August 2017 marked the beginning of the journey of the NSEC e-cell with the induction seminar and overwhelming response from students and faculties.It was an interactive seminar between the students, guests, and the organizer. Eminent personalities from successful startups and premier e-cells came down to present their vision to enhance a better understanding of entrepreneurship, work of e-cell and the whole ecosystem.

Faculty panel

The esteemed faculty of NSEC are greatly encouraging our endeavors and are supporting us by providing us with their valuable time advice and network in order to spread our vision throughout the campus and outside it. Alumni resource panel

Some of our alumni are well placed In prestigious positions in esteemed organizations or successfully running their Startups or businesses. Some are into research or pursuing higher studies in prestigious institutions throughout the globe they are and will be helping and guiding us with all their knowledge experience resources and valuable advice ultimately building a brighter future.

Executive committee

Core committee consists of a team of enthusiastic students as visionaries who tries to build a healthy learning atmosphere among the students and enhance the startup culture in the college. Our entrepreneurship cell operates under several wings in order to strive towards excellence in respective fields.

E-cell Representatives

E-cell Representatives are a bunch of dedicated students from each department of the college who are the faces of the organization in their respective classes, departments and later to the outer world.

E-cell Interns

E-cell Interns are groups of students interested in the organization and its work, these interns are inculcated under the various wings of the organization and assigned specific tasks to get hands-on experience in different works of the organization and henceforth, an exposure to the industry.
